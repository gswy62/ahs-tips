American Heritage Tips

This is a website dedicated to helping out future American Heritage students with their classes. Teachers often run their classes in very different ways, so this website intends to offer some guidance on approaching these different courses in the best possible manner.

Here are a few general tips:

  • studying the day of an assessment often works
  • go to extra help!
  • the textbook is your best friend
  • use Harys Dalvi's grade calculator
